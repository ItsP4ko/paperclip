### Integration Bug

**Rate-limit health-skip path mismatch**
- **File:** `server/src/middleware/rate-limit.ts:19`
- **Current:** `skip: (req) => req.path === "/health" || req.headers.upgrade === "websocket"`
- **Problem:** Rate limiter mounted at Express root (`app.use(...)`) so `req.path` for health is `/api/health`, not `/health`. The skip condition never matches.
- **Impact:** Health endpoint will be rate-limited after 200 requests from the same IP within 15 minutes.
- **Affected:** HARD-01, DEPLOY-06
- **Fix:** Change to `req.path === "/api/health"` and update test.
