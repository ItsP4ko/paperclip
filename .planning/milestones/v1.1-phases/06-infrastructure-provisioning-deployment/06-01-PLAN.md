<objective>
Cap the postgres.js connection pool at 10 connections in createDb() to avoid hitting Supabase's pooler connection limit.

Purpose: Supabase free/pro tier limits total connections. The default postgres.js pool is unlimited. Without this cap, the Railway container may exhaust all available connections and cause intermittent failures for other services or the SQL editor.

Output: Modified `packages/db/src/client.ts` with `{ max: 10 }` option passed to `postgres()`.
</objective>
