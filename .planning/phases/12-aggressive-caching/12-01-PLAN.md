<objective>
Add per-query `staleTime: 120_000` (2 minutes) to the three issue-related page queries so that navigating back to a previously-visited page renders data instantly without triggering a background refetch.

Purpose: CACHE-01 requires issue lists to be cached 2 minutes; CACHE-02 requires issue detail to be cached 2 minutes. The global staleTime is 30 seconds which causes unnecessary background refetches on every remount after 30s. Raising staleTime per-query to 120 000 ms gives a 2-minute fresh window where TanStack Query serves cached data with zero network activity.

Output: Three production files updated with staleTime, three test files created/extended to verify the configuration.
</objective>
